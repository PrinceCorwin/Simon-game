How to Play
Click start and the RuneStone will light up a rune in sequence, beginning with a pattern of only one light. Click on the corresponding rune to complete the pattern. Then the Runestone will light up two items. Click them in the correct sequence. On each turn, the RuneStone will add one to the pattern. You must memorize the sequence and click them in the corrrect order to win each turn. Game will end when you click a rune out of sequence. Score reflects the last sequence length completed.
